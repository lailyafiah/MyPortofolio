About Me

I would like to introduce myself. My name is Nur Laily Afiah, you can call me Laily. I study at Bina Sarana Informatics University majoring in Information Systems The reason I took this major is because I love programming, especially for the user part. The programming language that I learned is Javascript so that the website I create looks more dynamic and interactive.

During college, I had an online business selling women's clothing, hoodies and crewnecks. Enjoys learning foreign languages, namely English and Japanese. In the last few months I have created an online bookstore application project, a laundry landing page, a laundry admin application, an online store landing page, and a printer rental application.
